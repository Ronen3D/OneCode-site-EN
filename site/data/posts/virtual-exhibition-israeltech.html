A new 3D virtual exhibition from Medium has launched. The exhibition allows industry professionals to tour the virtual exhibition space, which showcases booths and enriched information about companies in the fields of instrumentation and control, pumps and compressors, robotics, and more.

Visitors to the virtual exhibition are drawn into the world and feel as though they are touring a physical exhibition. They can even see other visitors moving through the exhibition and interact with them. At the various booths they can watch videos, read and download information, and sign up for additional information. The result is an immersive experience that drives sales, accessible to potential customers around the world.
