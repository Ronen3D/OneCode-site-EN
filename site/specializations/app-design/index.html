App Design & Architecture

  • App design is an early and critical stage in app development
  • App design needs to be precise, going into detail, yet comprehensive enough to answer questions that will arise along the way.
  • App design makes it possible to identify strengths and weaknesses at the right time, enabling the team to prepare accordingly and launch the app on time and within budget.
  • App design provides a shared foundation for everyone involved in the development effort — from entrepreneurs to development managers, developers, designers, and even investors.
Contact Us OneCode offers to build your app design with you — leaving no loose ends

Preliminary Phase

The first step in app design begins with analyzing the business environment and customer needs. To decide how to get there, you need to know where you're going: who is the target audience, who are the existing and potential competitors, where does the opportunity lie, and where are the chances of success slim.

Functional App Design

Here we examine what the app is supposed to do. We don't yet look at how it does it, but we decide what options it offers users. We divide the app's capabilities into core capabilities — the central features that can go live — and additional capabilities that can be added later. This gives us an initial development roadmap and helps us focus on what matters most.

Behind-the-Scenes Design

Only after we precisely identify the app's objectives can we confidently move on to defining the backend system. We determine what performance the app requires, what data is needed to run it, and how the management system will operate.

UI Design & Wireframe

A critical point in the development process. OneCode's UX/UI experts will build a Wireframe of the app — including the structure of the various screens and the user flow — based on the requirements we've defined. Through thorough work at every stage, they will create an app flow that guides users precisely to the goals we've set, keeps them engaged within the app, and ensures they don't get lost along the way.

Technology Stack for the App

Evaluating which technologies are best suited for development. For any given app, there are several viable technology options, each with its own advantages and disadvantages across factors such as development cost, time, performance, data security, stability, future support, and maintenance. The OneCode team brings experience across a wide range of technologies, so you'll receive a genuine and objective recommendation for the most appropriate choice.

Infrastructure & Architecture Planning

Now we're almost ready to begin development. The team will plan the overall architecture, including server-side structure, client-server interface, client-side structure, and more. We'll also define the project development phases and timelines.

Based on the design document, the development team can begin building an app that meets your goals and sets it up for success.
